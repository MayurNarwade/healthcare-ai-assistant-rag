APPOINTMENT SCHEDULING POLICY
Appointments may be requested through the patient portal, by telephone, or at the reception desk. New-patient visits are generally allocated 30–45 minutes and follow-up visits 15–20 minutes. Requests are not confirmed until the scheduling system issues confirmation.
Patients should cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before the visit when possible. Telehealth may be offered for eligible follow-ups, medication reviews, and results discussions. Physical examinations, procedures, vaccinations, or clinician-directed assessments may require an in-person visit.
