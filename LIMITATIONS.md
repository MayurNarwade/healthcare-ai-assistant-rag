# Limitations
- Synthetic and intentionally small knowledge base
- General-purpose embedding model
- Heuristic relevance threshold and confidence labels
- No reranker, hybrid lexical retrieval, OCR, authentication, or durable metrics
- Ollama availability and local hardware affect latency
- Mock appointment data only; no booking occurs
- No clinical validation, medical-device approval, or guarantee of hallucination elimination
- Privacy-conscious design does not itself establish HIPAA compliance

## Future improvements
Biomedical embeddings, BM25 hybrid retrieval, cross-encoder reranking, query rewriting, metadata filters, document versions, citation validation, expert evaluation, red-team testing, RBAC, audit logging, persistent monitoring, FHIR integration, authorized scheduling, GPU inference, and human review.
